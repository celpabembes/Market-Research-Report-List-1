<p><strong>The Packaging Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Hot-melt based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaging adhesives market is primarily divided into three types: water-based, solvent-based, and hot-melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly, using water as a solvent, making them ideal for food packaging. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding and are suitable for various substrates but can pose environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. Hot-melt adhesives are thermoplastic and solid at room temperature, known for quick bonding and versatility, making them popular in packaging applications requiring efficient and durable seals.</p></p>
