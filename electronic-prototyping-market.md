<p><p>The electronic prototyping market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on PCB prototyping and electronic product prototyping. PCB prototyping involves creating custom printed circuit boards for testing and validation of electronic designs, allowing engineers to refine their products before mass production. In contrast, electronic product prototyping involves developing complete electronic devices, incorporating housing, firmware, and user interfaces. Both types are essential for accelerating product development cycles, reducing costs, and ensuring that final designs meet performance and functionality standards.</p></p>
