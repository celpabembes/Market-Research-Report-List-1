<p><p>Butterfly valves are quarter-turn valves used to regulate or isolate the flow of fluids, gases, or solids in various pipelines. Their design features a disc positioned in the center of the pipe, which rotates to open or close the flow pathway, making them efficient for large diameter applications. They are widely utilized in industries such as water and wastewater, oil and gas, power generation, and chemical processing due to their versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>The Butterfly Valves Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
