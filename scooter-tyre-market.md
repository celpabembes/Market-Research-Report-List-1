<p><p>The scooter tyre market comprises three main types: bias type, radial type, and others. Bias type tyres feature layers of fabric that crisscross, providing good stability and affordability, though they may lack in fuel efficiency and durability. Radial type tyres have plies arranged radially, offering better grip, lower rolling resistance, and enhanced mileage. Other market options include specialty tyres designed for specific terrains or conditions, catering to niche demands, such as off-road or performance-oriented scooters.</p></p>
