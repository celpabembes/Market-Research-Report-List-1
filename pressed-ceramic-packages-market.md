<p><ul><li>Ceramic-metal Sealing (CERTM)</li><li>Glass-Metal Sealing (GTMS)</li><li>Passivation Glass</li><li>Transponder Glass</li><li>Reed Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressed ceramic packages are specialized enclosures used to protect electronic components. Ceramic-metal sealing (CERTM) utilizes a robust seal between ceramics and metals, ensuring durability. Glass-metal sealing (GTMS) combines glass and metal components, offering hermetic protection. Passivation glass enhances the stability of electronic circuits, while transponder glass is used in RFID technology for communication applications. Reed glass encases reed switches, allowing them to operate in various environments, making these packaging types critical for reliability in electronic devices.</p></p>
