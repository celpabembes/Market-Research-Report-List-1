<p><p>Enterprise Content Management (ECM) systems are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based ECM offers accessibility, scalability, and reduced IT management burdens by storing content in remote servers, allowing users to access data from anywhere. In contrast, on-premise ECM systems are hosted locally, offering greater control over data security and compliance but requiring significant investment in infrastructure and maintenance. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific needs for flexibility, control, and cost.</p></p>
