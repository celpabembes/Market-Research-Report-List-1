<p><p>The Bentonite powder market is primarily categorized into Sodium Bentonite and Calcium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite is characterized by its high swelling and adsorption properties, making it ideal for applications in drilling fluids, cat litter, and sealants. In contrast, Calcium Bentonite has lower swelling capacity and is often used in cosmetics, food processing, and pharmaceuticals due to its ability to absorb impurities. Both types serve diverse industries, driven by their unique properties and applications tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
