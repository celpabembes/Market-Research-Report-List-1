<p><p>Greenhouse films are essential in agriculture, utilizing various polymers to optimize plant growth. Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) is known for its flexibility and UV resistance, ensuring durability and longevity. Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) offers excellent stretchability and tear resistance, making it ideal for covering large areas. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is lightweight and cost-effective, providing effective protection against environmental elements. Together, these materials enhance crop yield, reduce energy costs, and improve sustainability in greenhouse operations.</p></p>
