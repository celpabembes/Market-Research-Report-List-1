<p><p>The Virtual Machine Backup and Recovery market presents two primary types: agent-based and agentless solutions. Agent-based backups require software agents installed on each virtual machine to manage data backup, providing granular control and advanced features but often demanding more resources and management. In contrast, agentless backups operate through the hypervisor, eliminating the need for individual agents, thereby simplifying deployment and reducing overhead. Each approach has its advantages, catering to different organizational needs and preferences in backup management.</p></p>
