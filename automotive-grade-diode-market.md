<p><p>The Automotive Grade Diode market encompasses various types, each serving specific functions in vehicle electronics. Automotive Schottky Diodes offer low forward voltage drop and fast switching, ideal for power management. Automotive Silicon Carbide Diodes enhance performance under high temperatures and voltages, suitable for electric vehicles.
