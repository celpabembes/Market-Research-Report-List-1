<p><p>The Wind Direction Sensor market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Photoelectric Wind Direction Sensor** utilizes light beams and optical principles to determine wind direction, offering high precision and reliability.</p><p>2. **Voltage Type Wind Direction Sensor** operates by measuring changes in voltage produced by the wind's effect on a resistive element, providing a straightforward and cost-effective solution.</p><p>3. **Electronic Compass Wind Direction Sensor** employs magnetic field data to determine direction, integrating advanced technology for real-time navigation and orientation, ideal for mobile applications.</p></p>
