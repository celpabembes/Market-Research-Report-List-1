<p><strong>The Blood Glucose Test Strips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glucose Oxidase</li><li>Glucose Dehydrogenase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood glucose test strips market is primarily categorized into two types: glucose oxidase and glucose dehydrogenase strips. Glucose oxidase strips employ an enzymatic reaction that produces hydrogen peroxide, indicating glucose levels, widely used for their accuracy. In contrast, glucose dehydrogenase strips utilize an alternative enzymatic reaction that does not require oxygen, allowing for stable readings in varying conditions. Both types cater to the growing demand for diabetes management, offering patients reliable tools for monitoring blood sugar levels.</p></p>
