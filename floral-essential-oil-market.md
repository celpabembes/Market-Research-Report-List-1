<p><p>The floral essential oil market is categorized into three main types: Sweet Floral, Herbaceous Floral, and Rich Floral. Sweet Floral oils, such as jasmine and ylang-ylang, are characterized by their light, airy scents and are often used in perfumes and aromatherapy. Herbaceous Floral oils, like lavender, blend floral notes with green, earthy tones, ideal for relaxation. Rich Floral oils, including rose and tuberose, offer deep, complex fragrances, making them popular in luxury products and high-end fragrances. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
