<p><p>Fluidized Bed Filters are advanced filtration systems that utilize the principle of fluidization to separate particles and contaminants from gases or liquids. In these systems, a fluid passes through a bed of solid particles, causing them to behave like a fluid, which enhances the contact between the fluid and the filter medium. This technology is increasingly adopted across various industries, including water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals, due to its efficiency in handling large volumes and varying particle sizes.</p><p>The Fluidized Bed Filters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.5% during the forecast period.
