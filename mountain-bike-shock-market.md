<p><ul><li>Air Shock</li><li>Coil Shock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mountain bike shock market primarily features two types: air shocks and coil shocks. Air shocks are lightweight and adjustable, using air pressure to tailor the ride feel, making them popular for cross-country and trail biking. Conversely, coil shocks utilize a metal spring, offering consistent performance and durability, ideal for downhill and enduro riding. Each type caters to different riding styles and preferences, balancing weight, adjustability, and responsiveness to enhance overall riding experience and performance on diverse terrains.</p></p>
