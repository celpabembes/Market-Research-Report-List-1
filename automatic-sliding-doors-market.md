<p><p>The automatic sliding doors market is categorized mainly into two types: bi-parting and single sliding doors. Bi-parting doors consist of two panels that slide open from the center, providing wider access, making them ideal for high-traffic areas such as shopping malls and airports. Single sliding doors have one panel that slides to one side, suitable for smaller spaces while maintaining ease of access. Both types enhance convenience and efficiency in entryways, promoting a seamless transition between indoor and outdoor environments.</p></p>
