<p><p>The UHP heat exchangers market is divided into two main types: metal shell and non-metallic shell heat exchangers. Metal shell heat exchangers are durable and suitable for high-pressure applications, often used in industries like chemical processing and oil refining. Non-metallic shell heat exchangers, made from materials like polymer composites or glass, offer advantages such as corrosion resistance and lightweight properties, making them ideal for sensitive environments like pharmaceuticals and food processing where contamination risks must be minimized.</p></p>
