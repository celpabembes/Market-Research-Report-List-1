<p><strong>The Blood Irradiation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiation market comprises two primary types: X-ray blood irradiation and gamma-ray blood irradiation. X-ray blood irradiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to inactivate pathogens in blood products, enhancing safety and extending shelf life. In contrast, gamma-ray blood irradiation employs gamma radiation from isotopes like Cobalt-60 to achieve a similar effect, effectively targeting harmful microorganisms and reducing transfusion-related infections. Both methods are critical in blood safety protocols, ensuring that transfusions minimize the risk of disease transmission.</p></p>
