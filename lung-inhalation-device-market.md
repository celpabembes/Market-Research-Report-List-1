<p><p>The lung inhalation device market comprises three main types: compression nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and mesh nebulizers. Compression nebulizers use compressed air to turn liquid medication into aerosol for inhalation, suitable for various patients. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize high-frequency vibrations to create mist from the medication, offering quiet operation and efficient drug delivery. Mesh nebulizers employ a vibrating mesh to generate fine aerosol particles, enhancing medication absorption and portability. Each type serves distinct needs in respiratory therapy, catering to diverse patient requirements.</p></p>
