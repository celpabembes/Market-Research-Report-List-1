<p><strong>The Bluetooth Thermal Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal Thermal Printer</li><li>Thermal Transfer Thermal Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth thermal printer market comprises two main types: direct thermal printers and thermal transfer printers. Direct thermal printers utilize heat-sensitive paper that darkens when exposed to heat, making them ideal for short-term labeling and receipts without ink. In contrast, thermal transfer printers use a ribbon to transfer ink onto the substrate, providing more durable prints suitable for long-lasting labels and barcodes. Both types cater to various applications, including retail, logistics, and healthcare, offering wireless convenience through Bluetooth connectivity.</p></p>
