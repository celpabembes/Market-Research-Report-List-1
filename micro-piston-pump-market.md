<p><strong>The Micro Piston Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Miniature Axial Piston Pump</li><li>Miniature Radial Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro piston pump market is divided into two main types: miniature axial piston pumps and miniature radial piston pumps. Miniature axial piston pumps utilize pistons arranged parallel to the axis of the pump, offering high pressure and efficiency, ideal for applications requiring precise fluid delivery. In contrast, miniature radial piston pumps feature pistons arranged radially, allowing for compact designs and smoother operation, making them suitable for low-flow applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including medical, automotive, and industrial automation.</p></p>
