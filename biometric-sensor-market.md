<p><p>The biometric sensor market comprises various types, each utilizing distinct technologies. Capacitive sensors detect electrical changes due to skin contact, commonly used in fingerprint recognition. Ultrasound sensors employ sound waves for facial and voice recognition, providing depth data. Optical sensors capture light reflections for iris and facial detection. Thermal sensors analyze heat patterns for identification, particularly in security applications. Electric field sensors measure variations in capacitance to recognize gestures and proximity, enhancing user interaction in various devices.</p></p>
