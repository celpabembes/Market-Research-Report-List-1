<p><p>Ultraviolet ray intercepting glass is categorized into monolayer and double layer types based on their protective capabilities. Monolayer glass consists of a single protective coating designed to block UV rays, offering basic protection. In contrast, double layer glass features two coatings, enhancing its UV filtering efficiency and durability. This dual-layer design not only provides superior UV protection but also improves thermal insulation and reduces glare, making it ideal for various applications, including automotive, architectural, and specialty glass products.</p></p>
