<p><p>The Mobile LiDAR Scanner market comprises two primary types: mechanical and solid-state. Mechanical LiDAR scanners utilize rotating components to capture high-resolution 3D data, making them suitable for diverse applications such as topographical mapping and forestry. In contrast, solid-state LiDAR scanners lack moving parts, providing increased durability and compact designs. They are favored for automotive applications and environments requiring robustness. Both types cater to different industry needs, balancing factors like accuracy, speed, and system complexity.</p></p>
