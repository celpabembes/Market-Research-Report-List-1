<p><p>Architectural rendering software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed directly on users' local systems, providing greater control over data and performance but requiring significant hardware investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based rendering software operates online, enabling easy access from anywhere with an internet connection. This model offers scalability, lower upfront costs, and automatic updates, making it appealing for collaborative projects. Each type caters to different user needs and organizational structures.</p></p>
