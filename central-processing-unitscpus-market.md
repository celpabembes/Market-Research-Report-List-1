<p><p>The Central Processing Unit (CPU) market comprises various packaging types, including Ball Grid Array (BGA) and Chip Scale Package (CSP). BGA packages feature a grid of solder balls on the underside, allowing for better heat dissipation and robust connections, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, CSP provides a compact solution where the chip itself serves as the package, enabling miniaturization and efficient use of space for mobile devices. Both types are crucial for advancing CPU technologies.</p></p>
