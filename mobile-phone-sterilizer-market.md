<p><p>The mobile phone sterilizer market is primarily divided into two types: wand-type and enclosed sterilizers. Wand-type sterilizers utilize ultraviolet (UV) light to disinfect surfaces by manually sweeping the device over the phone, offering portability and convenience. On the other hand, enclosed sterilizers involve placing the mobile phone inside a designated chamber where UV light or other sterilization technologies eliminate germs effectively. Both types cater to the growing demand for hygiene and cleanliness in mobile device usage.</p></p>
