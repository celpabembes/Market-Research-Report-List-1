<p><strong>The Large-size TFT-LCD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TN</li><li>IPS</li><li>AFFS</li><li>MVA</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large-size TFT-LCD market includes several display technologies: </p><p>- **TN (Twisted Nematic)** panels are known for their fast response times and are cost-effective, but offer limited viewing angles and color reproduction.</p><p>- **IPS (In-Plane Switching)** panels provide superior color accuracy and wider viewing angles, making them ideal for professional use.</p><p>- **AFFS (Advanced Fringe Field Switching)** enhances color and viewing angles further than traditional TN.</p><p>- **MVA (Multi-domain Vertical Alignment)** panels excel in contrast and black levels. Other technologies include various emerging and niche displays catering to specific applications.</p></p>
