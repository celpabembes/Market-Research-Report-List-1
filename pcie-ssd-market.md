<p><strong>The PCIe SSD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MLC NAND</li><li>TLC NAND</li><li>3D NAND</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCIe SSD market features various NAND types, each catering to different performance and cost needs. MLC (Multi-Level Cell) NAND offers higher endurance and speed, suitable for enterprise applications. TLC (Triple-Level Cell) NAND, commonly used in consumer products, provides larger storage at lower costs but with reduced endurance. 3D NAND technology stacks memory cells in layers, enhancing capacity and performance while minimizing space. These types influence pricing, durability, and speed, shaping the competitive landscape of the SSD market.</p></p>
