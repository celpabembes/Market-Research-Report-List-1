<p><p>Library Preparation and Target Enrichment are essential processes in Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) that facilitate the accurate analysis of genetic material. Library preparation involves fragmenting DNA or RNA samples and ligating adapters, which are sequences that allow for the sequencing process. This is crucial for generating libraries of nucleic acids that can be amplified and sequenced. Target enrichment, on the other hand, focuses on isolating specific regions of interest from the genomic DNA, enhancing the efficiency and depth of sequencing by concentrating on relevant sequences.</p><p>The Library Preparation and Target Enrichment for Next Generation Sequencing Product Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period.
