<p><p>The paraffin wax emulsions market can be divided into two main types: non-ionic and ionic emulsions. Non-ionic emulsions are characterized by their stability and versatility in various applications, avoiding issues related to ionic interactions. They are often used in cosmetics, coatings, and food industries. Ionic wax emulsions, on the other hand, consist of charged particles that can enhance stability under certain conditions, making them suitable for specific industrial applications, such as textiles and adhesives, where ionic properties are beneficial.</p></p>
