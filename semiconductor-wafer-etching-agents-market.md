<p><p>The semiconductor wafer etching agents market comprises two main types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching involves the use of liquid chemicals to remove material from the wafer's surface, typically providing uniform etching and better control of profiles. In contrast, dry etching utilizes gases or plasmas to achieve a more precise and anisotropic etching process, essential for creating intricate features in semiconductor devices. Both methods are crucial for advancing semiconductor manufacturing technology and enhancing device performance.</p></p>
