<p><ul><li>IPV</li><li>OPV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polio vaccines market comprises two main types: Inactivated Polio Vaccine (IPV) and Oral Polio Vaccine (OPV). IPV is an injectable vaccine that provides robust immunity without the risk of vaccine-derived poliovirus, making it safer for immunocompromised individuals. OPV, administered orally, is easier to distribute and effective in creating herd immunity through community vaccination efforts. Both vaccines play critical roles in global polio eradication efforts, with IPV being preferred in routine immunization and OPV used during mass vaccination campaigns.</p></p>
