<p><strong>The Aircraft Cooling Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Cooling Turbine</li><li>Water Cooling Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft cooling turbine market comprises two main types: gas cooling turbines and water cooling turbines. Gas cooling turbines utilize gas flow to dissipate excess heat, making them suitable for high-performance engines where weight is a critical factor. In contrast, water cooling turbines employ water as a coolant to absorb and transport heat away from the engine components, offering effective cooling in various environmental conditions. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimal engine temperature and enhancing overall aircraft performance.</p></p>
