<p><ul><li>Contact Thermistor Temperature Sensor</li><li>Non-contact Thermistor Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermistor temperature sensor market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact thermistors. Contact thermistor temperature sensors measure temperature by direct contact with the object, providing high accuracy and rapid response times, making them ideal for applications like HVAC and industrial processes. In contrast, non-contact thermistor temperature sensors utilize infrared technology to gauge temperature from a distance, suitable for applications where physical contact is impractical, such as in medical devices and non-invasive testing. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
