<p><strong>The Anticoagulant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral</li><li>Injectable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anticoagulant market can be divided into two primary types: oral and injectable anticoagulants. Oral anticoagulants, such as direct oral anticoagulants (DOACs) and vitamin K antagonists, offer convenient administration and are commonly used for long-term management of conditions like atrial fibrillation and thrombosis. Injectable anticoagulants, including low molecular weight heparins and direct thrombin inhibitors, are typically used in hospital settings for immediate effect in acute situations. Both types play crucial roles in preventing and treating thromboembolic disorders.</p></p>
