<p><p>The Patient Temperature Management market is divided into two main types: Patient Warming Systems and Patient Cooling Systems. Patient Warming Systems are designed to maintain or elevate a patient's body temperature, preventing hypothermia during surgeries or critical care, often utilizing blankets, radiant heat sources, or warm air devices. Conversely, Patient Cooling Systems are used to reduce body temperature in cases of fever, heat stroke, or during specific procedures, employing cooling blankets or ice packs to enhance patient safety and comfort.</p></p>
