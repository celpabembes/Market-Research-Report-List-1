<p><p>3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems are essential in the electronics manufacturing industry for quality control. The market is divided into two main types: Inline 3D AOI and Offline 3D AOI. Inline 3D AOI systems are integrated into the production line, providing real-time inspection and enabling immediate feedback during the manufacturing process. In contrast, Offline 3D AOI systems operate separately from the production line, allowing for batch inspections and more detailed analysis, enhancing overall product quality and reliability.</p></p>
