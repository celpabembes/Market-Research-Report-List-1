<p><ul><li>Force Feedback Haptic Glove</li><li>Precision Hand Tracking Haptic Glove</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The haptic glove market is primarily divided into two types: Force Feedback Haptic Gloves and Precision Hand Tracking Haptic Gloves. Force Feedback Haptic Gloves utilize motors and actuators to simulate tactile sensations and resistance, enhancing user immersion in virtual environments. In contrast, Precision Hand Tracking Haptic Gloves focus on accurately capturing finger and hand movements without necessarily providing tactile feedback. These gloves are used in applications ranging from gaming and virtual reality to rehabilitation and training simulations, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
