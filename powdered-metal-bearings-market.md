<p><strong>The Powdered Metal Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Sleeve Bearings"</li><li>"Flange Bearings"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powdered metal bearings market is categorized into two primary types: sleeve bearings and flange bearings. Sleeve bearings, which are cylindrical in shape, provide support for rotating shafts and are commonly used in applications requiring low friction and high load capacity. Flange bearings, on the other hand, feature a protruding flange that aids in mounting, offering stability in applications where space constraints or alignment issues may arise. Both types capitalize on the benefits of powdered metal technology, such as improved wear resistance and manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
