<p><p>The ostomy products market primarily consists of one-piece and two-piece bag systems. One-piece bags integrate the pouch and skin barrier into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use, making them suitable for patients who prefer minimal handling. In contrast, two-piece systems have a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing for flexibility in changing pouches without replacing the barrier. This option caters to users seeking convenience and comfort, accommodating varying lifestyles and preferences in ostomy care.</p></p>
