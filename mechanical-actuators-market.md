Linear actuators convert rotational motion into linear motion, ideal for applications requiring precise straight movement, such as in robotics and manufacturing. Rotary actuators, on the other hand, produce rotational movement, widely used in valve control and robotics. Other actuators may include specialized options like piezoelectric or electro-mechanical types, catering to niche applications in aerospace, automotive, and healthcare industries.</p></p>
