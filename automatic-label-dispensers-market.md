<p><p>The automatic label dispensers market is categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic label dispensers. In contrast, fully automatic label dispensers operate independently, applying labels at high speeds without human assistance, suitable for large-scale production environments. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in labeling processes, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
