<p><p>The High Temperature Copper and Aluminum Foil Tape market comprises two primary types: Aluminum Tapes and Copper Tapes. Aluminum Tapes are lightweight, durable, and resistant to moisture, making them suitable for insulation and electrical applications. They are often preferred for thermal shielding and heat reflection. Copper Tapes, on the other hand, offer superior conductivity and are commonly used in grounding and EMI/RFI shielding applications. Both types cater to industries requiring high-temperature resistance and performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
