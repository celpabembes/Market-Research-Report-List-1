<p><p>The mountain bike front fork market primarily consists of two suspension types: air spring and coil spring forks. Air spring suspension forks utilize pressurized air to provide adjustable stiffness and weight savings, offering a more responsive ride tailored to varied terrain. Conversely, coil spring suspension forks use metal coils for consistent performance and durability, typically favored for their reliability and ease of maintenance. Both types cater to different rider preferences and biking styles, influencing performance and comfort on challenging trails.</p></p>
