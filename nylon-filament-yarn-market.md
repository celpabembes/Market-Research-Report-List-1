<p><p>The nylon filament yarn market consists of various types, including Flat, POY (Partially Oriented Yarn), FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn), and HSO (High-Speed Oriented). Flat yarn is typically used in applications requiring wide surfaces. POY is partially stretched, ideal for further processing into textile products. FDY, fully drawn and orientated, is suitable for high-quality fabrics. HSO is engineered for high-speed production processes, enhancing efficiency in manufacturing. Each type serves distinct applications in textiles, industrial uses, and fashion industries.</p></p>
