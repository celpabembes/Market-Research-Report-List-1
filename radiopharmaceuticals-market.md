<p><strong>The Radiopharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diagnostic Radioisotopes</li><li>Therapeutic Radioisotopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiopharmaceuticals market is categorized into two primary types: diagnostic radioisotopes and therapeutic radioisotopes. Diagnostic radioisotopes are used in imaging techniques, such as PET and SPECT scans, to visualize organ function and detect diseases early. In contrast, therapeutic radioisotopes are utilized for treatment purposes, delivering targeted radiation to destroy cancerous cells while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue. This dual functionality enhances patient care by facilitating accurate diagnosis and effective treatment options in nuclear medicine.</p></p>
