<p><p>The solid organ transplant immunosuppressant market comprises three main types based on chemical composition. First, chemical agents include synthetic drugs, such as corticosteroids and calcineurin inhibitors, which suppress the immune response to prevent organ rejection. Second, fungal products encompass immunosuppressants derived from fungal sources, like cyclosporine. Lastly, biologics consist of therapeutic products made from living organisms, including monoclonal antibodies that target specific immune pathways, offering more refined immunosuppression to enhance transplant success and reduce complications.</p></p>
