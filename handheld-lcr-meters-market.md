<p><p>The handheld LCR meters market is segmented based on measurement types: inductance, capacitance, and resistance. Inductance measurement focuses on assessing the ability of a component to store energy in a magnetic field, commonly used in coils and transformers. Capacitance measurement evaluates a component's ability to store electrical energy in an electric field, crucial for capacitors. Resistance measurement determines the opposition to current flow in a circuit, essential for analyzing conductors and ensuring circuit integrity. Each type serves specific applications in electronics and electrical engineering.</p></p>
