<p><p>The Rotary Coded Switches market is primarily categorized into two types: SMT (Surface Mount Technology) Type and Through Hole Type. SMT Type switches are designed for automated assembly, offering compact designs ideal for modern electronics with high-density circuit boards. Conversely, Through Hole Type switches provide robust physical connections suitable for larger components, often preferred in applications requiring enhanced durability and stability. Both types serve diverse applications across consumer electronics, industrial equipment, and automotive sectors, catering to varying design and assembly needs.</p></p>
