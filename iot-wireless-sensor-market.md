<p><p>The IoT wireless sensor market comprises various technologies, notably traditional wireless and LPWANs (Low Power Wide Area Networks). Traditional wireless technology, such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, offers short-range communication with higher data rates, ideal for applications requiring quick connectivity. In contrast, LPWANs are designed for long-range connectivity with low power consumption, making them suitable for remote monitoring and IoT deployments where devices transmit small amounts of data infrequently. Both technologies address different needs within the expanding IoT landscape.</p></p>
